## Overview

Sofile helps content creators with managing their media files across different cloud storage providers. It allows users upload the results of their work to the cloud storage, render and export files in different formats, download and import assets with one click without leaving the Adobe Creative Cloud application they are working in.

## My Impact

I have been working on lots of features for Sofile, implementing them by myself and leading junior developrs too. As a result, Sofile was totally revamped. A new billing strategy more comfortable to users, storage insights and cost optimizations, global-wide index search. All the features I worked on made this a beautiful and strong product.
