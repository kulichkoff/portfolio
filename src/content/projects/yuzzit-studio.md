## Overview

Yuzzit Studio is a browser video editor for media professionals who need to produce and distribute content beyond live streams. Editors work with clips and scenes, animated text templates, and social-format previews so finished videos are ready to publish without leaving Yuzzit’s ecosystem

## My Impact

I implemented some Studio features and helped with crafting a smaller UX-friendly Yuzzit Studio version. I have been working a long with template-driven text and customizable subtitle styles. My expertise helped align implementation with product goals for media content production.
